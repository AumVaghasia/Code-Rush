Each player has two knights, and they begin the game on the squares between their rooks and bishopsâ€”i.e., at b1 and g1 for White and b8 and g8 for Black. The knight has the trickiest move, an L-shape of two steps: either two squares vertically and then one square horizontally or two squares horizontally and then one square vertically. A knight at e4 could move to f2, g3, g5, f6, d6, c5, c3, or d2. The knight has the unique ability to jump over any other piece to reach its destination. It always moves to a square of a different colour.
